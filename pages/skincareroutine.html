In this section you will find how to make a skincare routine based on your skincare type, those are

  • Normal skin
  • Dry skin
  • Oily skin

Description

Normal skin
In This type of skin the sebaceous glans and the sudoripanous glands make equal amounts of substances, making skin looks healthy, like baby skin.

Dry skin
in this type of sky the sebaceous glans dont produce enough substance so the skin looks dry.

Oily skin
In the last type the sebaceous glans produce more substance than they should so the skin looks shiny.

Normal skin

Dry skin

If you have dry skin you should use products that occlusive your skin to keep the wet, try rich moisturizers and hialuronic acid, this acid can hold the moistness in your skin.
